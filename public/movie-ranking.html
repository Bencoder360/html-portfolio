The Best Movies According to Benjamin

My top 5 movies over the past five years.

Twenty Four

This is my favorite action movie. I love Jack Bauer's personality.

Transformers

If you love cars and aliens, then this is the movie for you. Action packed!

Passion of Christ

As a Christian this was one of the movies that made me understand the vicarous sufferings Jesus had to go through just to save the world. Amazing movie.

Extraction

Action packed and lots of shootings. It's a must watch for action lovers

Prison Break

Last but not least is Prison Break. You can't but admire Michael's ingenuity and how the team worked together to break away from prison.
